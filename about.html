About CataFoods

Discover the authentic flavors of Nigeria with our traditional cuisine

Our Story

Founded in 2015, CataFoods began as a small family-run restaurant in Lagos with a passion for bringing authentic Nigerian flavors to food lovers everywhere. Our founders, the Johnson family, wanted to share the rich culinary heritage of Nigeria with both locals and visitors.

Over the years, we've grown from a single location to multiple branches across Nigeria, while maintaining our commitment to quality ingredients and traditional cooking methods passed down through generations.
